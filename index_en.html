JS Group Ltd.

JS Group Ltd. specializes in the development and application of silicone, rubber, plastics, and their composite materials, and facilitating the development of all types of production mold and mold making tools. Our business encompasses a wide range of products, including automotive components, industrial parts, cell phones, computers, laptops, home appliances, eco-friendly products, and women’s and children’s products.

JS Group Ltd. emerged as a prominent enterprise in the electronics industry with its assembly technologies. The addition of silicone and rubber product manufacturing in China has been in operation since 2006 and continues today. The Group established Kunshan Ju Shuo Electronics Co., Ltd. in December 2013 and Chongqing Yang Shuo Electronics Co., Ltd. in April 2014 in order to promote the globalization of the Group. The Group currently employs more than 300 employees in the department of management, manufacturing production, technology engineering, quality control, and business promotion. Headquartered in Taiwan, the Group has strongholds in Kunshan and Chongqing.
